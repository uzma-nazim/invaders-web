Welcome to Irrelevant invaders



Irrelevant invaders is a 10k artistic project with a community thats built on combining our experience on NFT’s and for joining our community we want to give back not just our experience but also Eth for supporting us we want to reward our community we plan to raffle off eth for each trail map goal we hit dont miss out on your chance to become a irrelevant invader!

Irrelevant invaders is a community built of people who truely look into the future and see nfts for what they are! We wanted to build a community that supports one another in this trek into the new age of art collecting. We understand that most people are unexperienced in NFT’s thats why we ask you to join us in this journey and like we plan to support our community we will also be donating to the charity of our communities choice later on as part of one of our trail map goals. We plan to make a game and future collections as well as a merch line later on!
